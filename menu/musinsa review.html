MUSINSA

About MUSINSA
MUSINSA is Korea's largest online select shop which is including over 3500 of street fashion brands.
It is top player of fashion select shop market - with yearly turnover of 900billion won and 6millions of members.
It provides various services :
MUSINSA Standard (PB)
WUSINSA (for women)
MUSINSA Studio (shared office)
etc.

Features & Functions
The biggest advantage is that many services are available through a single app called MUSINSA.
If you click M button at the bottom center of the app, you can see all the services provided by MUSINSA at a glance. (except 'soldout' service)
Instead of having to install multiple apps for each service, it provides an integrated UX from one app to satisfy customers.

Two Points to Improve
1. Lack of App Optimization
Sometimes the app is slow to operate, or screen switching, scrolling, etc. are unnatural. As there are many services in a single app, it needs to be optimized so that slow speeds do not interfere with UX.
2. No Add Button on Product List Page
When a user looks at the product list page, he/she can see how many people have added the product to their list of interests, but to add it to his/her list of interests, he/she needs to click the product to enter the product detail page. If users can click the heart button directly on the list page to add it to their list of interests, it will be a smoother shopping experience.
